# Contributing

## Ways to contribute

You can contribute by:

- proposing benchmark items
- reviewing existing items
- suggesting taxonomy changes
- reporting ambiguity, leakage, or licensing issues
- improving documentation or reviewer guidance

## Item contribution expectations

Contributed items should include:

- a clear mathematical statement
- the intended taxonomy path
- `MSC2020` codes
- a problem type and answer format
- success criteria
- provenance and licensing notes
- a canonical solution sketch or outline

## Review expectations

Item proposals are not accepted automatically. Public benchmark content requires expert review and editorial signoff.

## What not to submit

Please do not submit:

- open conjectures
- copyrighted material without permission or clear transformation rights
- pure literature lookup prompts
- contest-style items unless they serve a clear research-style benchmark purpose
- model-generated items without human mathematical validation

## Feedback

If you are unsure whether an idea fits the project, open a discussion or use the feedback channels documented in [`community/feedback-channels.md`](community/feedback-channels.md).
