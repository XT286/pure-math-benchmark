# Proof Rubric

## Dimensions

Score each dimension from `0` to `4`.

- `main claim correctness`
- `logical validity`
- `use of hypotheses and definitions`
- `completeness`
- `proof strategy quality`
- `partial progress value`
- `communication clarity`

## Interpretation

- `4`: excellent
- `3`: strong with only minor issues
- `2`: mixed or partially correct
- `1`: weak but not entirely empty
- `0`: missing or mathematically invalid

## Fatal flaw guidance

Fatal flaws include:

- false central conclusion
- invalid key inference
- theorem application with missing essential hypotheses
- a proof skeleton that cannot be repaired locally

## Overall recommendation

After dimension scoring, choose one:

- `accept`
- `minor_revision`
- `major_revision`
- `reject`
